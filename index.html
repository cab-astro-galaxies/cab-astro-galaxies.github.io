Formation and Evolution of Galaxies

Our group investigates the properties of galaxies in the local and distant Universe using X-ray, ultraviolet, optical, infrared, sub-millimeter, and millimeter observations and theoretical models. In particular, our group has a strong interest in the study of the first galaxies and the properties of the early Universe, being deeply involved in the exploitation of the James Webb Space Telescope, the most powerful observatory ever built.
